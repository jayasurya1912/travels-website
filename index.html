ABOUT US

We Help Businesses Grow Faster

Mrs. Ranjani Sudhakar, the Proprietor of 7 Horses Tours & Travels, brings over 10 years of extensive experience in the transportation field. A diplomat in fashion design technology, Mrs. Sudhakar began this venture in 2014 with a single vehicle. Under her leadership, the company has grown significantly, now operating a fleet of over 10 buses and maintaining strong partnerships with numerous transport providers.

7 Horses Tours & Travels is a well-established transportation and tour operating company based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded in 2014, we have quickly built a strong reputation for providing reliable, comfortable, and customer-centric travel solutions for a diverse clientele. Since 2017, we have expanded our services to include dedicated school and staff transportation.

With a strong emphasis on punctuality and professionalism, 7 Horses Cabs has built a reputation for on-time service and transparent operations. We believe that trust is earned through consistency, which is why we focus on maintaining high service standards across all our cab offerings. From booking to drop-off, we ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.
